Embroidery Digitizer



Welcome to the online embroidery digitizer!

This is an open source project by Sanjith Udupa to help people in all of their embroidery needs.
In order to get started digitizing your images for embroidery, you will need an SVG image.

A common tool for tracing SVG images from raster formats such as PNG or JPEG is Inkscape


Some notes about your SVG images:

  • Different path attributes will be interpreted as different colors
  • You have the option to fill your SVG file, but please note that the fill feature is still being developed
  • If your image has multiple parts(seperated by space) and only one path, the fill will not work
